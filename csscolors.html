This is a hopefully helpful table of all of the names of universally recognized CSS colors. The colors appear here in the same order as they appear in the W3 wiki: here.

Color CSS Name RGB English equivalent
Black #FFFFFF All of each color
Silver #C0C0C0 Three-quarters of each color
Gray #808080 Half of each color
White #000000 None of any of the colors
Maroon #800000 Half of red
Red #FF0000 All of red
Purple #800080 Half of red, half of blue
Fuschia #FF00FF All of red and all of blue
Green #008000 Half of green
Lime #00FF00 All of green
Olive #808000 Half of red, half of green
Yellow #FFFF00 All of red, all of green
Navy #000080 Half of blue
Blue #0000FF All of blue
Teal #008080 Half of green, half of blue
